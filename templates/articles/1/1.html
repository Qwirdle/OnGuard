1.1 Importance of Cybersecurity in the Workplace

The first thing to understand when it comes to the importance of cybersecurity in the workplace is that the employees, like you, are the weakest link in the company's cybersecurity. This is because while computers and servers can be set up to be secure using software and configuration, people can’t be, at least not in a surefire way like that. This is because we are humans, and we make mistakes, but when it comes to your cybersecurity, these mistakes can cause both major harm to yourself, but also the company you work for. However, this course aims to educate and train you in order to be more secure, so that hackers and criminals can’t exploit you.

https://www.stealthlabs.com/

You might be thinking, “I don’t need to worry about cybersecurity, I’m not someone of importance in my workplace”, but you could not be more wrong. As an employee, you almost always have a good amount of access to your company's data and systems, and this is what hackers and criminals want. Many companies have been brought down by crippling cyber attacks, which is happening more and more over the years as the world becomes more integrated into technology, and this means that cyberattacks are becoming more and more of a threat. This is why it is of the utmost importance that you know how to stay secure.

t’s also important to note that you won’t just be at risk from malware going through your system within itself, but there are intricate types of attacks like Phishing that more directly or sometimes directly communicate with you through email, phone calls, or other methods in order to trick you into giving hackers and criminals access to your company’s data. The umbrella term for this is sort of psychological attack is called social engineering.

Summary

  • Cybersecurity is important for all employees

  • You have access to sensitive data and systems

  • Not all attacks are technical, some are psychological, known as social engineering attacks
