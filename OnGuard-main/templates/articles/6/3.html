Companies don’t like it when you keep secrets from them. This is especially true when the secret is an issue you are withholding from them.

Issues take lots of time to deal with, especially if they are related to a complicated system or something that requires approval or even legal documents like permits or court orders to change.

By reporting issues early you:

  • Let people who resolve those issues have more time to work on and plan around the issue

  • Minimize damage by alerting customers/users on potential insecurities.

      Certain features may be removed temporarily as a “lockdown” to prevent further issues until the issue is fixed

  • Increase trust (withholding issues from users is often scandalous)

  • Allow number of victims to be tracked

  • Allow the root of the issue to be found

      The root may be in a log file, log files are typically deleted after some time, making the root of the issue harder to find

  • Give less time for bad actors to use vulnerability to their advantage

  • Avoid being fired

Summary

  • Report issues quickly

  • This gives more time to work the issue out

  • This gives less time for the issue to cause problems
